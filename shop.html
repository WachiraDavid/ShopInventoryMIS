enlighten shop inventory MIS

Here's what you can do

1. Stock your shop

This feature allows you to add new purchases/items to the already exising stock

2. Make a sale

Through this feature, you get to slect from a list of stocked items as you add to customers' cart.

The system then gives you a list of all the items you're selling and computes the total selling price for you.

3. Inventory

This feature allows you to track the progress of your stock.

You can get information such as;

  • Stock sold
  • Remaining Stock

4. My Statements

This feature allows you to view your statements and download them where need be

5. Advertice your products

Through this platform, you can also advertice your products via the enlighten website at a very affordable cost.

6. System tutorial

Here, you get a detailed and easy to understand tutorial on how to use the enlighten shop inventory MIS.
