About Enlighten Shop Inventory MIS

Enlighten's Shop Inventory Management Information System is a platform that helps its users (shop owners) by allowing them to: -

  • Record their sales
  • Record their purchases(stock)
  • Track their stock
  • Download profit and loss statements
  • Get allerts when they are running out of stock
